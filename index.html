Summer Math Camp for 8th grades

Best Summer Ever!

1-5 July 2023🌴

  • Register

📐💡 Unleash Your Inner Math Whiz! 🔢✨

 Mathpreneur, an exciting summer camp for 8th grade students, is a collaboration between ABB Tech and Azerbaijan's Ministry of Education. Dive into various mathematical topics through interactive workshops, group projects, and real-world applications. Join us and unlock your inner math whiz in a fun and supportive environment! 

  • ABB Tech Learn more

What will you gain upon completion of the program?

  • Enhanced Problem-Solving Skills
    Master efficient techniques to confidently solve complex math problems
  • Collaborative Learning Experience
    Forge lasting friendships while working together on group projects and activities
  • Real-World Math Connections
    Discover the fascinating applications of mathematics in everyday life and future career opportunities

Stages

  • 1

    Registration

    Fill necessary part of registration form. Write letter of motivation describing you are interested in our camp program.

  • 2

    Online test stage

    The first (Qualifying) stage will consist of an online exam. Participants will be provided 10 questions to be solved and submited within 4 hours after the start of exam.

    Each participant has only 1 attempt to start the solution.

  • 3

    Interview

    Students who have successfully passed the selection stages will be involved in intensive training which will be held at Guba Rixos Hotel on 1 - 5 July

  • 4

    Summer Camp
